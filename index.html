How to Play

Turn Flow

  • Roll 2 dice and move clockwise.
  • Passing GO grants $200.
  • Buy unowned properties when prompted.

Rent & Sets

  • Land on owned property? Pay rent.
  • Railroads scale with railroad count.
  • Owning a full color set doubles base property rent.

Special Spaces

  • Chance/Community cards can reward or charge cash.
  • Taxes feed the Free Parking pot.
  • Go To Jail sends your token directly to Jail.
