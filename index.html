How to Play

Turn Flow

  • Roll 2 dice and move clockwise. Passing GO grants $200.
  • Roll doubles to take another turn.
  • Three doubles in a row sends you straight to Jail.

Rent & Sets

  • Land on owned property? Pay rent.
  • Railroads pay $25/$50/$75/$100 by count owned.
  • Utilities pay 4× or 10× the dice (1 vs 2 owned).
  • Owning a full color set doubles base property rent.

Jail

  • Pay $50 bail, use a Get Out card, or roll for doubles.
  • 3 failed attempts forces $50 and you must move.
  • Sitting in Jail is safe — no rent risk while you wait.

Cards & Spaces

  • Chance & Community Chest are separate decks.
  • "Advance to..." cards trigger buy/rent at the destination.
  • Taxes feed the Free Parking pot, paid out on landing there.

Bankruptcy

  • Short on cash? Properties auto-sell to the bank at half price.
  • Only when assets run out are you eliminated.
